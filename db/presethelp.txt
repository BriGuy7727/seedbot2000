__Preset Commands:__

**!preset <name>** - rolls a seed from a user-preset flagset
**!add <name> [--flags <flags>] [--desc <description>]** - add a new preset
**!update <name> [--flags <flags>] [--desc <description>]** - updates an existing preset
**!delete <name>** - deletes a preset
**!shuffle** - rolls a seed from a randomly selected preset
**!my_presets** - get a list of your saved presets
**!pflags <name>** - get the flags for the specified preset

__Additional Arguments:__

**&loot** - this will randomize all enemy drop/steals except for MiaBs, dragons and statues
**&true_loot** - this will randomize **ALL** enemy drops/steals
**&steve** - everything is and always will be STEVE
**&paint** - randomize the sprites, portraits and palettes
**&dev** - rolls your seed using a dev version of the randomizer (use **!devhelp** for more info)
